My favoritemovies

I have watched a lot of movies, but few movies have stayed with me over the years as these have. Take a look at some of my favorite movies below.

1)Gadar : One of Sunny Deol's best movies, it is the story of a Sikh man who marries a Pakistani woman who crossed the border by mistake. They have a child but they are soon separated as the parents of the girl take her back home. How Tara, the protagonist reunites his family is the crux of this story.

2)Koi Mil Gaya: Rohit lost his dad in an accident when he was little, he was in the same car, in his mother's womb. Because of the accident, Rohit has a disability, even though he is older, his brain is the same as a little kid. How Rohit meets an alien, overcomes his problems and lets the alien go in a supreme act of self sacrifice is what makes this story great.

3)Lagaan : Aamir Khan plays Bhuvan, a farmer who lives in a village taxed heavily by the British. Based in pre-independence India, Bhuvan and his friends stake it all on a cricket match between themselves and the British, to waive off all their taxes, or'Lagaan'.

4)Dil Chahta Hai : A tale of three friends, with unique love lives, and unique ways of navigating said love lives. A path breaking film which represented the youth like they were, away from typical bollywood dramatic representation.

5)Dangal : A father wanted sons so they could carry his legacy as a wrestler, forward. However, he was blessed with daughters. How this family overcame societal taboo to sire multiple champion athletes is the story of Dangal.

6)Good Will Hunting : Will Hunting is a gifted but poor orphan who works as a Janitor at a university. He sneaks around solving complicated math on the boards for fun until one of the professors finds him out. FOr the first time in his life, Will has a choice, a means to get out of squalor and make a life for himself.
